What is Bronze? List alloying elements in bronze and their ratio(s)?

Bronze is an alloy consisting primarily of Cu, commonly with about 12% tin(Sn) and, often with the addition of other metals such as aluminium (Al), manganese(Mg), nickel (Ni) or zinc (Zn) and, sometimes non-metals or metalloids such as arsenic (As), phosphorus (P) or silicon (Si).
